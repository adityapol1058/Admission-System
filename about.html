About Sardar Patel Institiute of Technology

Keeping in view the growing needs of industry and society, we are committed to creating an environment, which will raise the intellectual and moral standards of our students. Our endeavor is to strive for the overall development of students, thereby enabling them to accept challenges.

The training & placement cell caters to the industrial training needs of students and also provides placement services to students through campus interviews. Many multinational and well-known Indian conglomerates regularly visit our campus every year for their requirement of high caliber human resources.
